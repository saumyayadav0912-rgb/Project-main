About Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the land of royals, is known for its majestic forts, vibrant culture, golden sand dunes, and rich traditions. From the palaces of Jaipur to the deserts of Jaisalmer, Rajasthan offers a journey back in time to India’s royal past.

Top Destinations

Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is home to Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and rich Rajasthani culture.

Jaisalmer

The Golden City famous for its sand dunes, camel rides, and the Jaisalmer Fort.

Udaipur

The City of Lakes, Udaipur boasts palaces, romantic boat rides, and scenic beauty.
